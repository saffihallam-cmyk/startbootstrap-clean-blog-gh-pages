Photography - Saffariah's Snapshots

One of my life goals is to be willing to try new things. I love being able to connect with people over shared experiences, interests, passions, and lifestyles. Some of these things are hard to learn, like an instrument or a sport! Some of them don't ever become easier for me, like running or painting. But one hobby that I decided to try, and that stuck with me, is photography. I love taking pictures and capturing the joy of a single moment, a memory that you can keep forever and ever. This past year, I decided to make photography a side hustle while I study hard in school and work another part-time job. I have been able to take pictures for weddings, family photos, maternity shoots, graduations, senior pictures, and mission pictures, and I hope to continue building a reputable base of images that people can find inspiration from. I started an instagram page dedicated to my pictures, which I have tried to stay active on. See my instagram here.

Fun Facts About Me

  • My love for photography started as I sought to capture macro-experiences in nature, like flowers blooming and birds flying off in the distance.
  • I love candid moments more than posed ones. I believe that imperfect pictures capture perfect happiness.
  • My go-to editing platform is Adobe Lightroom. Here's why:
    1. It has a user-friendly interface that allows me to quickly adjust exposure, contrast, and colors to enhance my photos.
    2. Lightroom allows me to save my edits as presets for future use, making it easy to create uniform looks across my portfolio.
    3. Lightroom's non-destructive editing means I can experiment with different edits without worrying about losing the original image quality.
    4. The software offers powerful organizational tools, making it easy for me to manage and categorize my growing photo library.
